Arash Jamshidi

I am an ELLIS Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Helsinki, with an exchange host at KTH Royal Institute of Technology. I have a master's in Artificial Intelligence with a focus on Theoretical Machine Learning from Sharif University of Technology. In my Ph.D., I am supervised by Kai Puolamäki and Aristides Gionis, and in my master's I was supervised by Mahdi Jafari Siavoshani. My research focuses on Machine Learning theory and Trustworthy Machine Learning.
